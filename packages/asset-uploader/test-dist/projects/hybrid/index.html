Hybrid

Hybrid hosts three different activities: accommodation, restaurant and spa. The concept is bands linked in perpendicular fashion by a central corridor extending into the landscape. Each of the bands has a unique atmosphere enhanced by the lighting. The landscape is an integral part of the building. Pools of water from outside are projected and become a cascade within the building. The light is diffracted by using fragments of stacked glass, resulting in the illusion of water in the exterior. The materiality is a combination of black and grey slate with glass.
